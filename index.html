CLIMATE

This project started as an attempt to create a logo that would eventually be used in the airport facilities. Each student was assigned a field of interest concerning the asia continent (such as population, geography, etc...) My topic was Climate.
Design is all about extremes. therefore, I looked for the most extreme climate state in asia that would inspire me the most. That’s when I came up with the Monsoon. BACK...

WHY UMBRELLA

When I’ve decided to use the Monsoon as my
concept, I had to try many different graphic
directions in order to describe the Monsoon.

Eventually I chose the umbrella because it was
the simplest most common way to describe rain.
Monsoon is not a storm with any specific traits
except heavy rain and the way it forms.

The way the Monsoon forms is demonstrated
by the unique scrolling of the website. BACK...

UMBRELLA IN THE AIRPORT

When you arrive in one of the Monsoon effect-
ed countries, the first thing you need to con-
cider is how to protect yourself against the rain.

The most basic item is an umbrella. My brand is
here to cover up for you. BACK...

THE STORE’S CONCEPT

We don’t just provide you the best umbrellas,
we are also here to inform you of when a mon-
soon strikes so you will never be surprised.

Our website tracks Monsoon all over the asian
continent and is provided by a special GPS
system to know exactly what airport you are at.

When it starts to pour, you will be the first one
to know. BACK...
